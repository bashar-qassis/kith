Features

Everything you need to stay close

Kith gives you a thoughtful toolkit for the relationships in your life — without selling your data to do it.

👥

Rich contact profiles

Addresses, relationships, pets, important dates, custom fields — capture the details that make people unique.

🔔

Smart reminders

Birthday alerts, stay-in-touch nudges, and custom recurring reminders so no one falls through the cracks.

📖

Personal journal

Record conversations, life events, and reflections. Build a living history of your relationships over time.

📷

Photo integration

Connect your Immich server to surface photos of the people you care about, right in their profile.

🔃

Import & sync

Bring your contacts from vCard, Monica CRM, or CardDAV. No lock-in, no walled gardens.

🔐

API & automation

Full REST API with bearer auth and cursor pagination. Build integrations or automate workflows your way.

Your data. Your server. Full stop.

Kith is designed to be self-hosted. Your relationships are too personal for someone else's cloud. Run it on your own hardware and own every byte.

✓
Docker or bare-metal deployment
✓
PostgreSQL — battle-tested, yours to back up
✓
Encrypted sensitive fields at rest
✓
No telemetry, no tracking, no analytics
# Deploy in minutes
$ docker compose up -d
✓ kith-web started on :4000
✓ kith-db (postgres:15) ready
✓ migrations applied (46 tables)
# That's it. You're done.
$ open http://localhost:4000
